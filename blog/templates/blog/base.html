About

IsraHelp.com YOU'RE NOT ALONE IsraHelp is an initiative to help upcoming and current Olim with navigating the complexity that is Israeli bureaucracy and day-to-day life in Israel. IsraHelp focuses on providing the most accurate and up-to-date information in every single matter that every Oleh and Olah go through. We will help Olim build up contacts in different parts of the country, while establishing a circle of like-minded friends, who have all been in the same boat as you .
